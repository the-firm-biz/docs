## How does AutoBoy work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Projects register">
    Projects register their launch, sharing their **deployment address and launch method** with AutoBoy.

    See: [Registering a  Token Launch](/register-a-launch)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Users create buy orders">
    Users browse registered projects and create buy orders for project any token they want: setting how much to spend and the highest market cap they're willing to buy at.

    See: [Creating Buy Orders](/buylisting)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Users fund their AutoBoy wallet with USDC">
    See: [Wallets & Funds](/wallets-and-funds)
  </Step>
  <Step title="AutoBoy buys the moment it launches">
    AutoBoy’s realtime indexer detects the launch and swaps each user’s USDC into the token's Uniswap pool according to their order parameters.

    See: [What happens when a project deploys their token?](/how-does-auto-boy-work)
  </Step>
</Steps>
