𝐀𝐮𝐭𝐨𝑩𝒐𝒚© by The Firm© is **the pre-launch order book for tokens** on Base, enabling users to **line up buy orders for a project's token _before the token even launches_**.

☞ [https://thefirm.biz/autoboy/welcome](https://thefirm.biz/autoboy/welcome)

It works with any launchpad.

- **For Buyers** — Buyers add projects to their ‘buylist,’ set their order parameters, and wait for the launch: AutoBoy executes their auto-buy _the moment the token goes live_.
- **For Projects** — AutoBoy is a brand new tool in your TGE toolkit. It means you launch with an order book of committed buys, it turns those buyers into distribution before & after TGE, and gives you real insights before you launch: who will buy, how much, and at what price.

## How does AutoBoy work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Projects register their upcoming token launch with AutoBoy">
    Projects share their launch details with AutoBoy and we guarantee support 

    See: [Registering a  Token Launch](/register-a-launch)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Users create buy orders for projects ">
    Users browse listed projects and create buy orders for projects whose token they want to buy when it launches, choosing how much to spend and the max market cap they’re willing to buy at.

    See: [Creating Buy Orders](/buylisting)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Users deposit USDC into their AutoBoy wallet.">
    See: [Wallets & Funds](/wallets-and-funds)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Projects launch their tokens, AutoBoy auto-buys them for users">
    When the project launches their token, AutoBoy indexes it _in real-time_ and automatically executes the buy according to each user’s buy order parameters.

    There are various ways projects can launch their token, and AutoBoy supports most of them:

    See: [Token Launch & Auto-buy Types](/token-launch-types)
  </Step>
</Steps>
