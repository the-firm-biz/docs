#### 𝐀𝐮𝐭𝐨𝑩𝒐𝒚© by The Firm© is the pre-launch order book for tokens on Base, enabling users to line up their buy orders for a token _before the token even exists_.

Buyers add projects to their ‘buylist,’ set their order parameters, and wait for the launch: AutoBoy executes the auto-buy on their behalf _the moment the token goes live_.

If you're a project launching a token, AutoBoy offers a brand new tool in your TGE toolkit. 

Build a pre-launch order book and see real demand for your token: who will buy, how much, and at what price.

## How does it work?

At a high level:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Projects register their upcoming token launch with AutoBoy">
    See: [Registering a Project Profile / Token Launch](/register-a-launch)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Users create buy orders by adding to buylist">
    Users browse listed projects and create buy orders by adding the projects to their buylist, choosing how much to spend and the max market cap they’re willing to buy at.

    See: [Creating Buy Orders](/buylisting)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Users deposit USDC into their AutoBoy wallet.">
    See: [Wallets & Funds](/wallets-and-funds)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Projects launch their tokens, AutoBoy auto-buys them for users">
    When the project launches their token, AutoBoy automatically executes the buy according to each user’s buy order parameters.

    There are various ways projects can launch, which lead to different auto-buy types with different user outcomes.

    See: [Token Launch & Auto-buy Types](/token-launch-types)
  </Step>
</Steps>
