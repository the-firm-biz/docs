---
title: "Wallets & funds"
---

Users deposit Base USDC to their AutoBoy wallet, which is a kernel smart wallet managed by Privy.

As a user, this allows AutoBoy to use your deposit to auto-buy on your behalf when the conditions you’ve set are met.

After tokens are bought, they are held in the same AutoBoy wallet.

In both cases, you can withdraw your deposit or your auto-bought tokens at any time.
