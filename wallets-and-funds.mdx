---
title: "Wallets & funds"
---

Your AutoBoy wallet is a kernel smart wallet, managed by Privy, that holds both your funds and the tokens AutoBoy buys for you.

## Funding your wallet

Deposit Base USDC into your AutoBoy wallet. You can fund it whenever you like — before you start buylisting, or when you place your first buy order (AutoBoy will prompt you if you haven't yet).

Your deposited USDC stays in your wallet until AutoBoy uses it to fulfil one of your orders, on your behalf, once that order's conditions are met.

## One balance, shared across orders

All your orders draw from the same pool of funds: your USDC isn't locked to a single order. But don't worry — AutoBoy lets you know each time it makes a buy, so you can top up your wallet again to be ready for your next auto-buy.

## After a buy

Tokens AutoBoy buys for you are held in the same AutoBoy wallet, alongside any USDC you haven't spent.

You can withdraw your USDC or your purchased tokens at any time.
