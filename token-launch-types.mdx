## What are the different kinds of token launch?

Project profiles show different labels depending on what AutoBoy knows about the project’s upcoming launch, and the way auto-buy orders will be executed.

<Tip>
  Projects: the right approach for your launch depends on your launch method and what you want for your future token holders.

  See also: [Registering a Project Token Launch](/register-a-launch)
</Tip>

<Card title="Open Market Buys" href="/open-market-buys">
  Default auto-buy behavior. AutoBoy detects the project’s token launch on-chain and executes user orders _against the new pool._
</Card>

<Card title="Dev Buys (sniper protection)" href="/dev-buys">
  AutoBoy deploys the project’s token itself, including a "dev buy" in the same transaction so buy orders execute _in Block Zero_.
</Card>

<Card title="OTC Buys" href="/otc-buys">
  AutoBoy brokers a single pooled buy directly with the project on behalf of its users.
</Card>

## The “Verified Launch” label

AutoBoy works with listed projects to ensure that it:

1. supports their [chosen launchpad](/supported-launchpads) and
2. knows the deployment address ahead of time.

When AutoBoy has these two things, it can guarantee support of the project’s launch and will give the project a ‘Verified Launch’ stamp.
