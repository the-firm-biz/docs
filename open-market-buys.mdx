---
title: "Open Market Buys"
---

AutoBoy indexes Base in realtime, identifying launches by all supported deployment methods (see [Supported launchpads](/supported-launchpads)).

When AutoBoy’s indexer detects a launch from a deployment address associated with a project, it queries which users have buylisted that project and then executes buys according to their parameters (see [how AutoBoy handles sniper taxes](/effective-mcap) and [how prices are reevaluated](/effective-mcap#when-its-recalculated)).
