---
title: "Open Market Buys"
---

The default auto-buy type: AutoBoy executes swaps on behalf of users on the open market.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Users create buy orders before the token exists">
    Buyers [add the project to their buylist](/buylisting), choosing how much to spend and their Max Price (MCap).
  </Step>
  <Step title="AutoBoy detects the launch">
    AutoBoy indexes Base in realtime and spots the launch from the registered project's deployment address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AutoBoy evaluates the price">
    AutoBoy continuously [recalculates the market cap](/mcap-calculation#when-its-recalculated) and compares it against what each buylister is willing to pay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AutoBoy executes the buy">
    When an order's conditions are met, AutoBoy buys on the user's behalf (see [order prioritization](/order-prioritization)).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tokens land in the user's wallet">
    The bought tokens sit in the user's [AutoBoy wallet](/wallets-and-funds), to withdraw anytime.
  </Step>
</Steps>
