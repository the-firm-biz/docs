---
title: "Open Market Buys"
---



AutoBoy indexes Base in realtime, identifying launches by all supported deployment methods (see[ Supported Launchpads](/supported-launchpads)).

When AutoBoy’s indexer detects a launch from a deployment address associated with a project, it queries which users have buylisted that project and then executes buys according to their parameters (see [How does AutoBoy handle sniper taxes when executing?](https://app.notion.com/p/How-does-AutoBoy-handle-sniper-taxes-when-executing-37d99c3686de8047a04ef9c45ea0ac85?pvs=21) and [How are token prices reevaluated?](https://app.notion.com/p/How-are-token-prices-reevaluated-37d99c3686de80108565e9823fbd80e3?pvs=21) ).
