---
title: "Order validity"
---

How long AutoBoy will keep trying to fill an order after the token launches.

After this period’s over, the order will be invalidated and AutoBoy will stop trying to buy the token for you.

If you set a max order validity period of 4 hours, for example, AutoBoy will monitor the token price and keep reevaluating whether to buy (executing once your buy conditions are met) for up to 4 hours after the launch, then invalidate the order so it doesn’t accidentally auto-buy later.

## If your order doesn't fill

If the price stays above your [Max Price (MCap)](/setting-max-price) for the whole validity window, the order expires unfilled and AutoBoy stops trying. Your USDC stays in your wallet.

## If the token never launches

Your order just waits — there's no pre-launch expiry, so it stays active until the token launches or you remove the project from your buylist. Your USDC isn't tied up in the meantime.
