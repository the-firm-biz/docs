---
title: "Order validity"
---

In short, this is how long AutoBoy will keep trying to fill an order after the token launches.

After this period’s over, the order will be invalidated and AutoBoy will stop trying to buy the token for you.

If you set a max order validity period of 4 hours, for example, AutoBoy will monitor the token price and keep reevaluating whether to buy (executing once your buy conditions are met) for up to 4 hours after the launch, then invalidate the order so it doesn’t accidentally auto-buy later.
