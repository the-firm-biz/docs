---
title: "Spend (USDC)"
---

The amount you want to spend on the project's token, measured in USDC.

AutoBoy can only spend USDC deposited to your AutoBoy wallet. See: [Wallets & funds](/wallets-and-funds)

At launch, AutoBoy spends up to your Spend amount, capped by your wallet balance (assuming your other order parameters are met).

## Not enough USDC?

AutoBoy spends as much of your balance as it can, up to your Spend amount.

## No USDC?

If your wallet is empty, no buy is attempted when the project launches.
