---
title: "Spend (USDC)"
---

The amount you want to spend on that project's token, measured in USDC.

AutoBoy can only spend USDC deposited to your AutoBoy wallet. See: [Wallets & funds](/wallets-and-funds)

---

## Not enough USDC in AutoBoy wallet

AutoBoy will use as much of your balance, up to your 'Spend' amount and try and buy the token (assuming other buy order parameters are met)

---

## No USDC in AutoBoy wallet

If your AutoBoy wallet is empty, no buy will be attempted when the project launches their token.
