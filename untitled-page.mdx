---
title: "Spend (USDC)"
---

The amount you want to spend on that project's token, measured in USDC.

AutoBoy can only spend USDC deposited to your AutoBoy wallet. See: [Wallets & funds](/wallets-and-funds)

---

## What if I have less USDC in my AutoBoy wallet than the spend amount?

If a project launches a token  - AutoBoy will use up to your 'Spend' to try and buy the token. 

---

## What if I have no USDC in my AutoBoy wallet?

If your AutoBoy wallet is empty, no buy will be attempted.
