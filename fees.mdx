## Protocol Fee

- AutoBoy charges **no fees** at this time.

## Gas Fee

- AutoBoy **sponsors your gas fees** on Base.

## Sniper Fees

- Executed swaps incur LP fees and sniper fees as defined by the pool.
- Sniper Fees are calculated every block and factored into the current token price when AutoBoy evaluates whether a user's order parameters are met. See [Market cap calculation](/mcap-calculation)
