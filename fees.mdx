## Platform Fee

- AutoBoy charges a **3% platform fee** for every successful buy.
- The fee is taken in the token you bought.
- The platform fee is taken after the swap, so it isn't part of the [effective market cap](/mcap-calculation) AutoBoy evaluates your order against.

## Gas Fee

- AutoBoy **sponsors your gas fees** on Base.

## Sniper Fees

- Executed swaps incur Sniper fees & LP fees as defined by the pool.
- Sniper & LP Fees are factored into the current token price when AutoBoy evaluates whether a user's order parameters are met. See [Market cap calculation](/mcap-calculation)
