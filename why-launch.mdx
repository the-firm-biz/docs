## **Monitor & build demand**

- **Build real demand** – **Launch with an order book of committed buys,** rather than praying your community shows up.
- **Buyer insights** – See who will buy your token, how much they will buy, and at what price.
- **Preview your launch** – See a simulation of your token’s market cap at launch, based on AutoBoy’s committed auto-buys.

## **Distribution**

- **Integrated in your funnel –** Funnel demand for your token launch to AutoBoy - turning latent community demand into committed buys. We advise projects share their AutoBoy link in the first reply on each of their social posts.
- **Your community signal-boosts your project –** AutoBoy generates custom sharing assets for your project and incentivizes users to share them, building additional distribution for your project and awareness of your token launch both before and after launch.
- **Spotlighting** – Certain projects are ‘featured’ by AutoBoy - driving further eyeballs towards your launch.

## **Quality holders**

- **Limited user base** – AutoBoy is in closed beta with a selected allowlist of high-signal users - the kind you want as early token holders.
- **Allowlist your buyers –** You may allowlist certain users to buylist your project, further increasing your control over your launch.
- **Locking and vesting** – You may lock and vest your buyers' tokens via a Sablier NFT if you use AutoBoy as the token deployer to execute a block-zero [dev buy](/dev-buys).
