---
title: "Registering a token launch"
description: "Creating a public profile for your project's upcoming token launch"
---

Registering creates a public pre-launch profile for your token, so AutoBoy users can buylist it and commit buy orders before you launch. You don't need every detail finalised.

## How registration works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send us your launch details">
    [Contact Jeffrey on Telegram](https://telegram.me/firmjeff?text=Hi%20Jeff%0AI%20have%20a%20project%20I%20would%20love%20to%20register%20with%20AutoBoy.%0A%0AProject%20Name%3A%0AProject%20URL\(s\)%3A%0AProject%20Details%3A%0AContact%20Email%3A%0AFounder\(s\)%20social%20account\(s\)%3A%0A%0A%5Binsert%20business%20sign-off%20here%5D) with your project details and whatever you know about your launch:

    ```text
    Hi Jeff
    I have a project I would love to register with AutoBoy.
    
    Project Name:
    Project URL(s):
    Project Details:
    Contact Email:
    Founder(s) social account(s):
    
    [insert business sign-off here]
    ```

    <Tip>
      You can also tell your agent to register   [via our REST API](https://docs.thefirm.biz/api-reference/projects/register-a-project)
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="We confirm your launch">
    We check that AutoBoy [supports your launchpad](/supported-launchpads) and knows your deployment address ahead of time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your profile goes live">
    Your profile is published so users can start buylisting your token — earning a [Verified Launch](/token-launch-types) stamp once both checks pass.
  </Step>
</Steps>
