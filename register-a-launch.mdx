## Why should I use AutoBoy for my token launch?

### **Monitor & build demand**

- Launch with an order book of committed buys, rather than praying your community shows up.
- See who will buy your token, how much they will buy, and at what price.
- See a simulation of your token’s market cap at launch, based on AutoBoy’s committed auto-buys.

### **Distribution**

- Funnel demand for your token launch to AutoBoy - turning latent community demand into committed buys (we advise projects share their AutoBoy link in the first reply on each of their social posts).
- Additional distribution for your project, and awareness of your token launch both before & after launch via AutoBoy’s custom sharing assets, which users are incentivised to share.
- Certain projects are ‘featured’ by AutoBoy - driving further eyeballs towards your launch.

### **Quality holders**

- AutoBoy is in closed beta with a selected allowlist of high-signal users - the kind you want as early token holders.
