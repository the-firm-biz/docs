---
title: "Setting your Max Price (MCap)"
---

This is the _maximum [effective market cap](/effective-mcap) you’re willing to buy the token at._ 

It’s effectively the highest price you’re willing to pay.

It doesn’t mean you _will_ pay this much to buy the token, it’s just the upper limit.

<Info>
  **ANY / NO LIMIT**

  Setting a Max MCap to ANY or NO LIMIT means AutoBoy will execute the buy as quickly as possible, regardless of price.

  Use this when you want to be sure to get in and aren’t price-sensitive.
</Info>

See also: [Learn more about price / market cap calculation](/effective-mcap)
