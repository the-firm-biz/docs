---
title: "Max Price (MCap)"
---

<Tip>
  AutoBoy calculates an [effective market cap](/mcap-calculation): factoring in all fees, sniper taxes etc. when evaluating a token's price
</Tip>

The 'max price' is **the maximum market cap you’re willing to buy the token at**.

i.e. The highest price you’re willing to pay for the token.

It doesn’t mean you _will_ pay this much to buy the token, it’s just the upper limit.

<Info>
  **ANY / NO LIMIT**

  Setting a Max MCap to ANY or NO LIMIT means AutoBoy will execute the buy as quickly as possible, regardless of price.

  Use this when you want to be sure to get in and aren’t price-sensitive.
</Info>
