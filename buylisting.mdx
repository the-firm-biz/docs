---
title: "Adding Projects to Your Buylist"
---

The buylist is the list of all the pre-launch projects that a user wants to auto-buy tokens from, essentiall listing the various buy orders the user has placed.

## How do users create Buy Orders?

As a user, when you add a project to your buylist, you effectively create a _buy order_ for that project's unlaunched token, which will be executed when the token launches.

For each project you buylist, you select the parameters of your buy order:

- How much USDC you want to spend on the token launch;
- The [maximum price (MCap)](/max-price-m-cap) you’re willing to pay;
- An [order validity period.](/order-validity)

AutoBoy will then prompt you to deposit USDC to your AutoBoy wallet if you haven't already.

## What happens when a project launches?

Depending on the [token launch type](/token-launch-types), AutoBoy will use the USDC in [your AutoBoy wallet](/wallets-and-funds) to attempt your auto-buy based on the parameters you set. 

Your buy won’t be executed until/unless these parameters are met.

See also: [Order Prioritization](/order-prioritization)
