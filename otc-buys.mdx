---
title: "OTC buys"
---

In this launch type, AutoBoy brokers a token purchase with the project directly.

AutoBoy is an inherently flexible system designed to solve problems on both sides of the token launching marketplace. 

Users want to participate in the best token launches on Base. 

Projects want good token holders, capital, and a launch that drives attention to their business.

Therefore, AutoBoy can negotiate with projects directly, making _a single pooled buy_ of the project’s token on behalf of its users.
