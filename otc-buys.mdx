---
title: "OTC Buys"
---

AutoBoy brokers a single pooled buy directly with the project, on behalf of its users.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Users create buy orders before the token exists">
    Buyers [add the project to their buylist](/buylisting), choosing how much to spend.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AutoBoy negotiates with the project">
    AutoBoy strikes a deal directly with the project on behalf of its committed buyers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AutoBoy makes a single pooled buy">
    Rather than many separate swaps, AutoBoy makes one pooled buy of the project's token.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tokens are distributed to users">
    Each user receives their share in their [AutoBoy wallet](/wallets-and-funds).
  </Step>
</Steps>

This suits launches where a project wants to place a block of tokens with quality holders directly.
